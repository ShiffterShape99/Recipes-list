How to make a Detroit Style Pizza

How long does it take to make it?

To explain it would take 1 hour and 30 mintues to prep it, then 15 mins of cooking, finally 1 hour of rising for a total of 1 hour 50 minutes

Tips and Tricks before making the the recipe:

  • If your fingers are sticking to the dough, you can just lightly grease them with some olive oil and it'll be stick-free!
  • Use a Darker pan of 9x13 baking pan which works better than a lighter colored pan of the similar size.
  • Be sure you're rising the dough in a warm place in your oven.
  • You really want to put the cheese and sauce all the way to the edge of the pan. This cheese gets baked onto the crust, resulting in the BEST tasting crust edges.
