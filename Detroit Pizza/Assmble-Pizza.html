To Assemble and Bake the Pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Uncover the dough and spread the pizza sauce on top of the pizza dough, smoothing it out until it reaches the edges. This pizza doesn't really have a crust exterior so be sure to take it to the edges too.
  3. Top it with cubed provolone (if using) and sprinkle with mozzarella. I like to add some cubes of the aged provolone close to the edge of the pan so it gets all crispy.
  4. Add the pepperoni cups on top and be sure to place them overlapping and very close together. It should look like a blanket of pepperoni.
  5. Transfer to the oven and bake for 7 minutes, rotate the pan 180 degrees. And then bake for an additional 7 minutes, until the edges are golden brown and the cheese is all melty and bubbling.
  6. Allow to cool for about 5 minutes and then transfer to a large cutting board. Slice it up with a sharp knife and serve!

After all of that, pat yourself on the back and enjoy you pizza. You Deserve it for making it well done.
